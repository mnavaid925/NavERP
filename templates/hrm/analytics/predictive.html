How these figures are calculated

Attrition-risk score (0-100, higher = more risk) is a transparent weighted sum — not a machine-learning model:

  • Tenure (0-30): under 1 year scores highest (30); 10+ years scores 0.
  • Attendance (0-25): from the trailing-90-day absence and late-arrival rates.
  • Recent leave (0-20): frequency of leave requests in the last 90 days.
  • Probation proximity (0-15): unconfirmed employees near their probation-end date.
  • Review gap (0-10): no completed performance review in the last 12 months.

Bands: Low under 25, Medium 25-49, High 50-74, Critical 75+.

Hiring-needs projection: Gap = Budgeted − Filled. Projected Next-Quarter Exits = trailing-12-month exits ÷ 4 (current run-rate). Net Hiring Need = max(0, Gap) + Projected Next-Q Exits.
